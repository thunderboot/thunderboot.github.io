Word Swiper Ultimate ("Game"), developed by Thunderboot ("we," "us," or "our"), is a mobile game. This Privacy Policy describes how we interact with user information through the use of third party services within the Game.

    The Game itself does not directly collect any personal information from users. However, the Game utilizes several third-party services which may collect the following information:

    • Personal Information: Personal details such as your name, email address and social media profiles.
    • Payment information: Information such as payment card details.
    • Gameplay Data: Information regarding your in-game activities, such as game progress.
    • Device Information: Details about the hardware you use, such as model, operating system, and IP address.
    • Location Information: General location data derived from your IP address or device settings.

  2. Use of information

    • Google: When you see ads delivered, they may collect information about your device and interests to serve you targeted advertising. Please refer to the Google Privacy Policy for details on the information they collect and how they use it.

We do not receive any personal information from these transactions beyond confirmation of a successful purchase. Please refer to the Unity Privacy Policy for details on the information they collect and how they use it.

    The Game does not directly store any personal information, we have no control over data retention practices of the third party services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

  4. Accuracy and removal of data

    • Google: To update or delete your data used by Google, use this link.
    • Unity: To manage or delete your data, use the Unity account tool.

  5. Children's personal data

    We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has made an in-app purchase through the Game, please contact Google or Apple following their support guidelines.

  6. Security of your personal data

    We use security measures made available to us by the applicable third party services when transferring relevant data. The measures we adopt are designed to provide a level of security appropriate for the degree of risk involved with processing your personal information.
