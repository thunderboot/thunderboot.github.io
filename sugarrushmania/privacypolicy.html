Sugar Rush Mania Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-04-01

Sugar Rush Mania ("Game"), developed by Thunderboot ("we," "us," or "our"), is a mobile game. This Privacy Policy describes how we interact with user information through the use of Unity Ads and Unity IAP within the Game.

Information Collected

Sugar Rush Mania itself does not directly collect any personal information from users. However, the Game utilizes two third-party services:

  • Unity Ads: When you see ads delivered by Unity Ads, they may collect information about your device and interests to serve you targeted advertising. Please refer to the Unity Ads Privacy Policy (https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy) for details on the information they collect and how they use it.
  • Unity IAP: If you choose to make in-app purchases through Unity IAP, your purchase information will be processed by Unity. We do not receive any personal information from these transactions beyond confirmation of a successful purchase.
Use of Information

We do not directly use any user information collected by Unity Ads or Unity IAP. However, we rely on anonymized data for:

  • Game Improvement: We may use anonymized gameplay data provided by Unity to understand user behavior and improve the Game.
Disclosure of Information

We do not disclose any user information to third parties beyond what is necessary for Unity Ads and Unity IAP to function within the Game.

Data Retention

As we do not directly store any user information, we have no control over data retention practices of Unity Ads and Unity IAP. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Your Choices
  • You can opt out of receiving targeted advertising from Unity Ads by following the instructions provided in their Privacy Policy.
  • You can control your device settings to limit the information collected by Unity Ads. For example, you can disable location services or opt-out of interest-based advertising.
Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has made an in-app purchase through Unity IAP, please contact Unity following their support guidelines.

International Transfers

By using Unity Ads and Unity IAP, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in accordance with their privacy policies. The data protection laws in these countries may differ from the laws in your country.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may make changes to this policy with or without notice to you, you are encouraged to review the policy regularly.

Contact

You can contact us at privacy@thunderboot.com if you have any questions about this policy.
